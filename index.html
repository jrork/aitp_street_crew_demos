Interactive Demo - Try Both Versions

Welcome! I'm developing an app to help our street crews manage vehicle loading and unloading. This system helps us track vehicles from show entry through to show exit and allows street crew members to update vehicle status in real-time. For this demo, imagine yourself working as street crew and seeing these pages on your personal phone. This should help us keep track of the vehicles easier than repeatedly asking for status over the radio.

I've created two different interface approaches and would love your feedback on which works better. Please try both versions and let me know your thoughts!

üöó How It Works

  • Vehicle Status: Shows which vehicles are waiting, holding, unloading, etc.
  • Color Coding: Each vehicle bar shows the booth section color
  • Actions: Tap a vehicle to see available actions (Send, Hold, Picture, etc.)
  • Live Timers: Track how long vehicles have been either waiting or unloading
  • Filtering: Filter vehicles by section color
